Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Welcome to Bubble Burst. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the game handles information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

Information We Collect

Bubble Burst does not collect, store, or share any personal information directly from users.

The game only uses internet access for displaying advertisements through Google AdMob.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect and process certain data, including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

This information is handled according to Google’s own privacy policies.

You can learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google Uses Information from Apps

Internet Permission

The app requires internet access solely for:

  • Loading and displaying AdMob advertisements

The game does not use the internet for:

  • User accounts
  • Data uploads
  • Tracking personal files
  • Collecting personal messages or contacts

Children’s Privacy

Bubble Burst does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so the information can be removed if applicable.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

These services may collect limited technical information required for functionality and advertising.

Data Security

We do not store user data on our servers. Since no personal data is collected directly by the app, data security risks are minimized.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through your GitHub profile or Play Store listing contact information.
